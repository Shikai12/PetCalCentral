Frequently Asked Questions

Real questions from pet owners. Real answers. No fluff.

Are the calculators accurate?

Our calculators use published veterinary formulas — RER (Resting Energy Requirement) for calories, AAHA/APOP guidelines for BMI, and Cell Systems epigenetic clock research for age conversion. They are accurate for general estimates but cannot replace a veterinarian's assessment of your individual pet. Your dog might need 10% more or less than the calculator says. That's why we always say: use this as a starting point for a conversation with your vet.

Is my pet's data stored on your servers?

No. All calculator data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. It never leaves your device. We cannot see it, access it, or store it. If you clear your browser data, your calculator history disappears. We don't even have a server to store it on. It's just you and your browser.

Do I need to create an account?

No. PetCalCentral requires no sign-ups, no accounts, no passwords, no email verification, no "create a free account to unlock this feature." I hate that stuff. You shouldn't need an account to figure out how much to feed your dog. Just open a calculator and start using it.

Are these calculators a replacement for veterinary advice?

Absolutely not. Our tools are for informational purposes only. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical advice, diagnosis, and treatment. If your pet is sick, call a vet. If your pet has an emergency, go to an emergency clinic. Do not use a website calculator to diagnose your pet. I am not a vet. I am an IT guy with a spreadsheet.

How do I clear my calculator history?

Each calculator has a "Clear History" or "Reset" button. Click it and all your saved calculations for that tool are gone. You can also clear your browser's localStorage entirely, which will remove data from all our calculators. Your data. Your control.

Why are some links affiliate links?

Some product links are affiliate links. If you buy through them, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps keep the site free. We only recommend products we've actually used on Gus or Mochi. See our Affiliate Disclosure for full details.

Can I suggest a new calculator?

Yes! Email us with your idea. If it's something I personally need (or enough people ask for it), I'll build it. I've built every calculator on this site because I needed it first.

Why is this site free?

Because I built it for myself first. Then I realized other people might find it useful. The hosting costs are covered by affiliate commissions and my own pocket. It's not a business. It's a side project that got out of hand.
