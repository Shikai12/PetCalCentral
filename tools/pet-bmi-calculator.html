How We Estimate BMI

This tool approximates the veterinary Body Condition Score (1-9 scale). We compare your pet's weight against breed-size reference ranges. A score of 4-5 is ideal — ribs easily felt but not visible, with a clear waist from above.

Estimate only. A veterinarian's hands-on assessment is the gold standard.
