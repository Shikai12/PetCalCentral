Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

1. What We Collect

Tools work in your browser. We don't see the numbers you type. Your pet's data stays on your device using localStorage — it never leaves your browser.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics for anonymous traffic patterns. This includes your IP address (anonymized), browser type, pages visited, and time spent. No personal information is collected.

Cookies: Minimal cookies for analytics and affiliate link tracking. You can disable them in your browser settings.

Email: If you contact us, we use your email only to reply. We do not add you to any mailing list.

2. What We DON'T Do

  • We don't sell, rent, or trade your information.
  • We don't store your pet's health data on our servers.
  • We don't send marketing emails.
  • We don't share your contact information with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: See Google's Privacy Policy.

Affiliate Links: Amazon and Chewy links may use cookies to track referrals. This is standard affiliate practice and does not affect your privacy.

4. Data Retention

Your calculator data is stored locally in your browser. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or using the "Clear History" button on any tool page.

5. Contact

Questions? privacy@petcalcentral.com
