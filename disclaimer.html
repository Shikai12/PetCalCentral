Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Not Veterinary Advice

All tools, calculators, articles, and content on PetCalCentral are for informational and educational purposes only. They are not a replacement for professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always Consult a Professional

If your pet is sick, injured, or showing signs of distress, contact a licensed veterinarian immediately. Do not rely on online calculators or blog articles to diagnose or treat medical conditions.

Emergencies

If your pet is experiencing a medical emergency — difficulty breathing, seizures, severe vomiting, bleeding, or inability to urinate — seek emergency veterinary care immediately. Do not wait for an online tool to tell you what to do.

Calculator Limitations

Our calculators use published veterinary formulas and guidelines (AAHA, APOP, etc.) but they cannot account for:

  • Individual medical conditions
  • Medications your pet may be taking
  • Breed-specific health considerations beyond general ranges
  • Your pet's unique metabolism or activity patterns
  • Recent changes in health status

No Liability

PetCalCentral and its author, Ethan Barrett, are not liable for any damages, injuries, or health outcomes resulting from the use of our tools or content. Use at your own risk. When in doubt, call your vet.

In short: I'm not a vet. I built these tools because I needed them. You should still talk to a real veterinarian about your pet's health.
